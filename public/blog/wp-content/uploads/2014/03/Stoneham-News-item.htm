Kitchens International win big at KBB Review Awards 2014

07 March 2014

Kitchens International, a Stoneham Kitchens appointed dealer, has scooped three amazing awards at this year’s KBB Review Awards show, hosted at the Hilton in Birmingham on 4 March 2014, including the biggest award of the night, Kitchen Retailer of the Year. With showrooms in Glasgow and Aberdeen, the retailer is definitely the place to go to buy beautiful Stoneham Kitchens in Scotland.

KBB Review is a leading publication in the kitchens and bathrooms industry, and Kitchen Retailer of the Year is the award to win. For Kitchens International, the judging process involved making it through two shortlists, plus a visit in person from the judges so they could consider every aspect of the business before making their decision; this shows that Kitchen International is truly an industry leader.

Andrew Davies of the KBB Review Awards said: “Kitchens International is a very well deserved winner of this coveted award. It is a fantastic business which truly understands the changing kitchen market and really puts its customers first. It has a really well thought through business plan which values the input of every single employee and it translates that into great products, great design and great service. I can’t wait to see what they come up with to defend their title next year."

Kitchen International Designers Kieron Bell and Ailsa Howard also walked away with Kitchen Designer of the Year for a project costing £30 - 50,000 and Kitchen Contract Designer of the Year respectively.

Working with only the finest designers, styling stunning kitchens, we’re proud to have Kitchens International as a registered Stoneham dealership. These awards are well deserved, and show the skill and attention to detail and customer service that the retailer provides.

Kitchens International MD Gerry Watson said: “These prestigious awards are a huge credit to the whole KI team from top to bottom. At KI we believe that teamwork is the key to us delivering our promise, and these awards are testament to the hard work, energy, talent and commitment of every single member of our staff. We are delighted to be recognised as one of the best in the whole UK kitchen market.”

Congratulations, Kitchens International!
